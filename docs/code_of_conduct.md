- workshops, meetings, and training events,

- gender identity and expression  
- level of experience  
- nationality  
- personal appearance

## üéØ Our Standards

### Examples of positive behavior

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of different viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing when mistakes are made
- Focusing on the collective benefit of the community

### Examples of unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Offensive verbal comments or imagery
- Personal attacks or insults
- Publishing private information without explicit permission
- Sustained disruption of discussions or meetings
- Intentionally harmful or deceptive actions
